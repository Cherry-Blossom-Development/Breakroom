Prosaurus Phase 2 Ideas:

Idea #1: Go over the original design documents and see what we missed

  Project management with many layers of communication, was one of the original design goals.  
  We have not explored this further however since it seems as though software development is 
  a waning industry with the rise of agentic coding.

  People and Entity focused application..  we have the people part, missing the entity

  AI agents to help make tickets better.  Basically, look at existing tickets and learn from 
  them.  Eliminate the need to fill out dozens of boxes every time (re SNOW)

  Marketing (internal tools to do this)

  Load Balancers

  Desktop Application

  Business Admin Interface.  We already have Admin Technical, we also pretty much have Admin 
  Personal (although that is not what we call it, this is effectively the User Settings 
  area). what is missing is the Business Entities admin interface.

  CDN for flat files (explore what we have on s3 and if this is sufficient)

  ETL and Data Performance and Data Reporting 

Idea #2: Add some metrics for users so they can see how many people viewed their 
  store/products etc…

Idea #3: Add usage metrics for users and/or admin.  The ability to see how many posts happen 
  per day/per user could be useful, also knowing how much storage a user is taking up might 
  be of interest.

Idea #4: Heatmap… where on the site are users going?  what features do they actually use.

Idea #5: Add people to Prosaurus without them knowing… What?  yes.. a way for the admin user 
  to put a particular email address in (probably someone we know) and get their account 
  effectively created but in a state where they have never actually logged in.  Doing this 
  will allow us to give a promotion (such as a free upgrade) to this user and then send them 
  an email about it.

Idea #6: Create a way for bands to have band websites, similar to how artists can have artist 
  websites
